About Hi, I’m Duda

I’m a software delivery and DevOps consultant focused on one thing: helping engineering teams ship high-quality software faster and with greater confidence.

Over the past decade, I’ve worked with organizations of all sizes—from early-stage startups to large enterprises—helping them untangle complex delivery pipelines, reduce lead time, and build a culture of continuous improvement.

I believe that great software delivery isn’t about tools alone. It’s about people, process, and practice. I partner with your teams to identify bottlenecks, establish metrics that matter, and implement sustainable practices that stick long after the engagement ends.

What I Do Services

Every engagement is tailored to your organization’s specific context. Here’s how I can help.

🗺️

Value Stream Mapping

We visualize your end-to-end delivery pipeline—from idea to production—identifying every handoff, wait time, and bottleneck. You’ll get a clear, actionable map of your delivery flow with prioritized improvement opportunities.

Lead Time Flow Efficiency Bottleneck Analysis
🚀

Deployment Pipeline Optimization

From slow, manual releases to fast, automated deploys. I help you design and evolve CI/CD pipelines that reduce cycle time, eliminate toil, and give every commit a clear path to production.

CI/CD Automation Cycle Time
🔒

Rollout Processes & Safety

Ship with confidence. We’ll implement progressive delivery strategies—feature flags, canary releases, blue-green deployments—and establish rollback mechanisms, observability checks, and deployment freeze alternatives.

Progressive Delivery Canary Releases Feature Flags
🧪

Testing Strategy & Coaching

I coach teams on testing techniques that actually move the needle: test pyramid optimization, contract testing, property-based testing, and building a culture where quality is everyone’s responsibility—not a bottleneck.

Test Pyramid Contract Testing Team Coaching
📊

DORA Metrics Improvement

We measure what matters: deploy frequency, lead time for changes, mean time to restore (MTTR), and change failure rate. Then we build a targeted improvement plan to move the needle on each metric.
